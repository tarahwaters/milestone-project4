✷ nyree waters ✷

artist / designer

Nyree is an abstract artist, specialising in collage.

Nyree grew up on the coast of North Wales before heading up to Scotland to study a degree in Illustration at Edinburgh College of Art. She currently lives in Manchester + works freelance as an artist / musician / creative facilitator.

* incase you’re wondering, it’s pronounced ‘n-eye-ree’

More About Nyree

✷ music / art / writing ✷

Sustainability is at the heart of everything she makes, using recycled materials + eco-friendly processes wherever she can.

Her visual style is playful + intuitive, flowing across a range of mediums from collage to painting.

A lover of words, Nyree uses poetry in both her artwork + music as a way of processing heavy thoughts/feelings with a gentle touch.
